Reuse is a candidate decision, not a canonical selection. Command Center’s integrated primitives have stronger runtime evidence; Enso’s policy components have stronger isolation and binding properties. Adapters and contract tests are preferred to wholesale copying. Existing Magna and Magna Enso remain evidence and reuse candidates; neither is classified as disposable, obsolete, or canonical-forward.

Hermes capabilities may continue evolving across later stages under the confirmed human decision, but current evidence remains 0/6 active capability families in Enso.
